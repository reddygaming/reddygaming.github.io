Hardware Recommendations

The right gear can make all the difference. Here's what pros use to get an edge on the competition.

Monitor

A high refresh rate is crucial for a smooth, responsive experience.

  • Alienware AW2521HFL (240Hz)
  • BenQ ZOWIE XL2546K (240Hz)
  • Asus ROG Swift PG259QN (360Hz)

Mouse & Keyboard

Lightweight mice and low-latency keyboards are key for speed and precision.

  • Mouse: Finalmouse Starlight-12, Logitech G Pro X Superlight
  • Keyboard: Wooting 60HE, Razer Huntsman Mini

Controller

For controller players, a high-quality, responsive controller can be a game changer.

  • Battle Beaver: Known for custom buttons and triggers.
  • SCUF Reflex: High-performance controller with remappable paddles.
  • Xbox Elite Series 2: Highly customizable with interchangeable components.

PC Components

The core of your system. A powerful CPU and GPU are essential for top performance.

  • CPU: AMD Ryzen 7 7800X3D, Intel Core i7-13700K
  • GPU: NVIDIA RTX 4070 or better
  • RAM: 32GB DDR5 @ 6000MHz+

Pro Player Settings

Copy the settings used by professional Fortnite players to gain a competitive edge.

Peterbot

  • DPI: 800
  • In-Game Sens: 8.0% X / 8.0% Y
  • ADS Sens: 40.0%
  • Keybinds: Wall: V, Ramp: F, Floor: R, Cone: C, Edit: Left Shift

Sensing

  • DPI: 800
  • In-Game Sens: 8.0% X / 8.0% Y
  • ADS Sens: 30.0%
  • Keybinds: Wall: C, Ramp: E, Floor: Q, Cone: L-Shift, Edit: F

DeRoller

  • Look Sens: 48% Horizontal / 48% Vertical
  • ADS Sens: 10% Horizontal / 10% Vertical
  • Build Multiplier: 2.0x
  • Button Layout: Custom (Edit: L-Stick, Jump: R-Stick)

Vadeal

  • Look Sens: 45% Horizontal / 45% Vertical
  • ADS Sens: 11% Horizontal / 11% Vertical
  • Build Multiplier: 2.1x
  • Button Layout: Custom (Edit: L-Stick, Jump: A)
